Correlates entity geolocation observations with satellite-based Internet Service Provider usage patterns. Identifies entities using satellite connectivity (e.g. Starlink, Hughes, Viasat) and maps their digital footprint to physical locations.

⊞ About Anomalies

Burst Entities: Entities generating an abnormally high number of events (μ + 3σ above the mean). Often indicates automated polling, misconfigured hardware, or active tracking beacons.

High Speed / Bad Coordinates: Implausible physics or errant GPS device hardware.

Weak Unit Association: Devices with very low confidence scores mapping them to a military unit/site.
